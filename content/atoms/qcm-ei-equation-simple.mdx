<Question>
La solution de $2x + 6 = 0$ est :
</Question>

<Option>$x = 3$</Option>
<Option correct>$x = -3$</Option>
<Option>$x = 6$</Option>
<Option>$x = -6$</Option>

<Explanation>
On resout l'equation en isolant $x$ : $2x + 6 = 0$ donne $2x = -6$, donc $x = \frac{-6}{2} = -3$.
</Explanation>
