<Question>
Que vaut $2^{-3}$ ?
</Question>

<Option>$-8$</Option>
<Option>$-6$</Option>
<Option correct>$\frac{1}{8}$</Option>
<Option>$\frac{1}{6}$</Option>

<Explanation>
$2^{-3} = \frac{1}{2^3} = \frac{1}{8}$
</Explanation>
