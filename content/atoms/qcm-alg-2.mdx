<Question>
La factorisation de $x^2 - 16$ est :
</Question>

<Option>$(x - 4)^2$</Option>
<Option>$(x + 4)^2$</Option>
<Option correct>$(x - 4)(x + 4)$</Option>
<Option>$(x - 8)(x + 2)$</Option>

<Explanation>
$x^2 - 16 = x^2 - 4^2 = (x - 4)(x + 4)$ (difference de carres).
</Explanation>
