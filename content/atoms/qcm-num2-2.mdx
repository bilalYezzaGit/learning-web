<Question>
$\sqrt{72}$ simplifie vaut :
</Question>

<Option correct>$6\sqrt{2}$</Option>
<Option>$8\sqrt{2}$</Option>
<Option>$36\sqrt{2}$</Option>
<Option>$2\sqrt{18}$</Option>

<Explanation>
$72 = 36 \times 2$, donc $\sqrt{72} = \sqrt{36} \times \sqrt{2} = 6\sqrt{2}$.
</Explanation>
