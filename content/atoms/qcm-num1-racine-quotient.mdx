<Question>
$\dfrac{\sqrt{50}}{\sqrt{2}}$ est egal a :
</Question>

<Option>$\sqrt{48}$</Option>
<Option correct>$5$</Option>
<Option>$\sqrt{25}$</Option>
<Option>$25$</Option>

<Explanation>
On utilise la propriete $\dfrac{\sqrt{a}}{\sqrt{b}} = \sqrt{\dfrac{a}{b}}$ : $\dfrac{\sqrt{50}}{\sqrt{2}} = \sqrt{\dfrac{50}{2}} = \sqrt{25} = 5$.
</Explanation>
