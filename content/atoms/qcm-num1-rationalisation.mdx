<Question>
$\dfrac{4}{\sqrt{2}}$ apres rationalisation vaut :
</Question>

<Option>$\dfrac{4\sqrt{2}}{4}$</Option>
<Option correct>$2\sqrt{2}$</Option>
<Option>$\dfrac{4}{2}$</Option>
<Option>$4\sqrt{2}$</Option>

<Explanation>
On multiplie le numerateur et le denominateur par $\sqrt{2}$ :

$$\dfrac{4}{\sqrt{2}} = \dfrac{4 \times \sqrt{2}}{\sqrt{2} \times \sqrt{2}} = \dfrac{4\sqrt{2}}{2} = 2\sqrt{2}$$
</Explanation>
