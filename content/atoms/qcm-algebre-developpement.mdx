<Question>
$3(2x - 4)$ est egal a :
</Question>

<Option>$6x - 4$</Option>
<Option correct>$6x - 12$</Option>
<Option>$5x - 1$</Option>
<Option>$6x + 12$</Option>

<Explanation>
On applique la distributivite : $3(2x - 4) = 3 \times 2x - 3 \times 4 = 6x - 12$.
</Explanation>
