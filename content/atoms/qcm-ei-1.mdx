<Question>
La solution de $2x + 4 = 0$ est :
</Question>

<Option>$x = 2$</Option>
<Option correct>$x = -2$</Option>
<Option>$x = 4$</Option>
<Option>$x = -4$</Option>

<Explanation>
$2x = -4$, donc $x = -2$
</Explanation>
