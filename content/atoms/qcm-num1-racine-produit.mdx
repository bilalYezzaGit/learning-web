<Question>
$\sqrt{3} \times \sqrt{12}$ est egal a :
</Question>

<Option>$\sqrt{15}$</Option>
<Option correct>$6$</Option>
<Option>$\sqrt{36}$</Option>
<Option>$36$</Option>

<Explanation>
On utilise la propriete $\sqrt{a} \times \sqrt{b} = \sqrt{a \times b}$ : $\sqrt{3} \times \sqrt{12} = \sqrt{3 \times 12} = \sqrt{36} = 6$.
</Explanation>
