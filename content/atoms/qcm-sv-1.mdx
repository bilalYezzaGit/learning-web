<Question>
La relation de Chasles stipule que :
</Question>

<Option>$\vec{AB} + \vec{BA} = \vec{0}$</Option>
<Option correct>$\vec{AB} + \vec{BC} = \vec{AC}$</Option>
<Option>$\vec{AB} - \vec{BC} = \vec{AC}$</Option>
<Option>$\vec{AB} = \vec{BC} + \vec{CA}$</Option>

<Explanation>
La relation de Chasles : pour tous points A, B, C, on a $\vec{AB} + \vec{BC} = \vec{AC}$.
</Explanation>
