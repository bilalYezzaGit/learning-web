<Question>
$2^5$ est egal a :
</Question>

<Option>$10$</Option>
<Option>$25$</Option>
<Option correct>$32$</Option>
<Option>$64$</Option>

<Explanation>
Par definition, $2^5 = 2 \times 2 \times 2 \times 2 \times 2 = 32$.
</Explanation>
