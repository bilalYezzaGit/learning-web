<Question>
Si $\cos(\alpha) = 0{,}6$ et $\sin(\alpha) = 0{,}8$, que vaut $\tan(\alpha)$ ?
</Question>

<Option>$\frac{3}{4}$</Option>
<Option correct>$\frac{4}{3}$</Option>
<Option>$1{,}4$</Option>
<Option>$0{,}48$</Option>

<Explanation>
$\tan(\alpha) = \frac{\sin(\alpha)}{\cos(\alpha)} = \frac{0{,}8}{0{,}6} = \frac{4}{3}$
</Explanation>
