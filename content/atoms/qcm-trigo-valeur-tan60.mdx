<Question>
$\tan(60°) =$
</Question>

<Option>$\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}$</Option>
<Option>$1$</Option>
<Option correct>$\sqrt{3}$</Option>
<Option>$\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}$</Option>

<Explanation>
$\tan(60°) = \sqrt{3}$.

On peut retrouver cette valeur en utilisant $\tan(\alpha) = \frac{\sin(\alpha)}{\cos(\alpha)}$ :

$$\tan(60°) = \frac{\sin(60°)}{\cos(60°)} = \frac{\sqrt{3}/2}{1/2} = \sqrt{3}$$
</Explanation>
